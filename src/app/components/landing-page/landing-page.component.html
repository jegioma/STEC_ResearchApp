Welcome to the STEC ResearchApp!

The STEC research opportunities prepares students with a better understanding of the research process and the scientific method. They also provide students with a strong background for STEC courses and other research opportunities.
Select the resources button to access the following information:

  1. Open the "STEC Mentors_Topics" spreadsheet to browse research projects, keywords, and faculty mentors for current opportunities.
  2. Connect with faculty research supervisor for permission to participate.
  3. Read STEC course syllabus.
  4. Complete STEC Faculty/Student Research Agreement.
  5. Email questions to STECcoordinator@ggc.edu.
